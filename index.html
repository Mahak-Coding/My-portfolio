About Me.

I’m Mahak Tittal, a self-taught Front-End Developer, Web Designer, and Video Editor passionate about crafting clean, responsive, and user-focused digital experiences. During my internship at Nathoasto, I worked directly with clients, led solo tasks, participated in strategic meetings, and delivered work under tight deadlines — gaining real-world experience in communication, leadership, and project ownership. I specialize in Shopify theme customization, WordPress websites, and creating visually engaging content that aligns with brand goals. With a strong eye for design and detail, I aim to build solutions that are not only functional but also impactful.
