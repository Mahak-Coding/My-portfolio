About Me.

Design decisions matter when they’re tied to real outcomes.

I work on Shopify and e-commerce experiences that prioritize clarity, usability, and performance—built to scale with the business behind them. Every layout, interaction, and custom feature is approached with intent, balancing visual precision with practical execution.
